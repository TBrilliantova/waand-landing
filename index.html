FAQ

Is this secure?
Your inputs are used to run the agents and nothing else. We don't train on your work or share it with third parties. If you have specific security requirements, reach out and we'll give you the details.
What if it doesn't work for me?
We stand behind the work. If waand isn't the right fit after you've tried it, reach out — we'll figure out next steps together. No lock-in from the waitlist.
How do I get started?
Join the waitlist above. When early access opens in March, we'll email you with everything you need. No installs or configuration required — waand runs in your browser.
Does it work for complex, multi-flow products — not just single screens?
Yes. Each agent handles the full scope of your brief — multi-step flows, complex state logic, and system-wide design decisions. It's built for real products, not toy demos.
